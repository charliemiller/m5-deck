
Charlie Miller

Charlie is the founder and managing director of M5 Advisors. With a background in digital strategy, product development, and user experience, Charlie helps real estate leaders integrate technology to enhance experiences, optimize operations, and maximize asset value.

Before founding M5 Advisors, Charlie was VP of Innovation & Digital Experience at Brookfield Asset Management, where he focused on digital master planning for large-scale developments. Prior to that he was Senior Director at Intersection (part of Sidewalk Labs and Alphabet), where he shaped smart city technology initiatives; and a partner at Control Group, the technology and design consultancy behind projects like the LinkNYC smart city network. For two decades his work has centered on reimagining the interaction between people, technology, and urban spaces — an approach that continues to influence his work today.

In addition to his work with CRE developers and landlords, Charlie serves as a board member of the Governors Island Foundation, supporting the transformation of this historic site into a dynamic public destination. He is also an active advisor to several real estate technology startups.

Charlie has advised organizations like the Related, Caruso, and the NFL on technology-driven placemaking and consumer engagement. A recognized industry voice, he frequently shares insights on how emerging technologies are transforming commercial real estate.
