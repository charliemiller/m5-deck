01

The Logo

The M5 mark is the primary identifier of the firm. Applied consistently with adequate breathing room. Always used as-supplied — never recreated, retyped, or redrawn.

Primary — black background
On white — secondary use
← min. clearance on all sides →

Clear Space

Always maintain a minimum clear space equal to the height of the "5" letterform (~25% of logo height) on all four sides of the mark.

This protected zone ensures the mark reads cleanly and is never crowded by adjacent text, imagery, or page edges.

Minimum reproduction size: 32px / 0.45in

Logo Don'ts

Don't stretchNever alter proportions in any direction
Don't rotateThe mark is always upright
Don't use on colored backgroundsBlack or white only

04

Brand Photography

M5 photography communicates a specific viewpoint: the built environment as system, and people as its purpose. Preferred imagery is aerial — looking down from above at the geometry of cities, the flow of people through space, and the scale of real estate decisions. Images should feel authoritative, not decorative.

Select imagery that is

Aerial or elevated
Drone, rooftop, or high-angle perspectives that reveal spatial systems
Human-present
People visible in the landscape — walking, gathering, moving through space
Desaturated or muted
Cool or neutral tones; avoid vivid saturated color processing
Geometrically compelling
Grid patterns, intersections, radial layouts — structure as composition

Avoid imagery that is

Generic office interiors or boardroom clichés
Posed "handshake" or stock-looking business photography
Highly saturated or HDR-processed real estate renders
Abstract tech imagery (glowing networks, circuit boards, data streams)
Street-level "busy city" photos without spatial context

Preferred imagery

People visible within
the built environment

Aerial crosswalk — geometry of movement
Urban intersection top-down — city as system
Residential street network — infrastructure at scale

Image treatment

When placing photography in documents or presentations, apply a subtle desaturation (15–25%) and slight contrast boost. Never apply colored filters, vignettes, or brand-color overlays except when used as a full-bleed background with a dark overlay for text legibility.

05

Voice & Tone

M5 operates where real estate, technology, and finance intersect. Our voice: precise without being cold, confident without being promotional, expert without being inaccessible.

We are

Direct

We state positions clearly. Advisory value comes from clarity, not qualification.

Credentialed

We reference real projects. Specificity builds trust — Hudson Yards, LAX, ICD Brookfield Place Dubai.

Multilingual

We translate between real estate operators, telecom providers, and technology partners.

We are not

Promotional

We don't describe ourselves in superlatives. Results speak — adjectives don't.

Vague

Phrases like "strategic solutions" and "best-in-class" are avoided. We name specific disciplines and outcomes.

Jargon-heavy

We use technical language precisely — not to signal expertise, but when it is the clearest word available.

Write this

"M5 serves as owner's representative and infrastructure strategist for large-scale development — translating fiber, DOOH, and connectivity decisions into owner value."

Not this

"M5 Advisors is a premier boutique firm offering innovative, best-in-class digital advisory solutions for the real estate industry."
