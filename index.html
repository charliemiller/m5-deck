05 — Leadership
Charlie Miller
Founder & Managing Director
With a background in digital strategy, product development, and user experience, Charlie helps real estate leaders integrate technology to enhance experiences, optimize operations, and maximize asset value.
Before founding M5 Advisors, Charlie was VP of Innovation & Digital Experience at Brookfield Asset Management. Prior to that he was Senior Director at Intersection (part of Sidewalk Labs and Alphabet), and a partner at Control Group — the technology and design consultancy behind projects like the LinkNYC smart city network. For two decades his work has centered on reimagining the interaction between people, technology, and urban spaces.
